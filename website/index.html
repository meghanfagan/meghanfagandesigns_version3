About Meghan

Hello! My name is Meghan Fagan. I currently live in Kansas City, MO, and my passions are web, graphic, and user experience design.

I discovered the joy of creating websites in middle school. I began with the "classic" website platforms like Expages.com, but soon moved to creating the code myself.

I took advanced art classes in high school, but coding slowly fell off my radar. As time passed, life, family, and work became my priorities. Eventually, however, I decided to go back to school and return to what I loved when I was younger - creating.

So much about design has changed from when I was younger, but that is what I love about it - there is always something new to learn, and it always keeps me on my toes!

Design Philosophy

I am obsessed with web, graphic, and user experience design. My personal design style leans towards clean and modern design, with a touch of whimsy here and there.

I love creating websites and designs that are easy to use and figure out! There's always something new to learn about design, which is why I am always on the lookout for anything that will help me grow as a creator!
